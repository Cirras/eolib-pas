@shorttitle(EOLib)
@title(Introduction)

EOLib is a Pascal library for writing applications related to the classic MMORPG Endless Online.

It offers comprehensive support for the EO network protocol and data files, and is largely generated
from the @url(https://github.com/cirras/eo-protocol EO Protocol) specification.

Compatible with @url(https://www.embarcadero.com/products/delphi Delphi) (2009+) and
@url(https://www.freepascal.org/ FreePascal) (3.1.1+).
